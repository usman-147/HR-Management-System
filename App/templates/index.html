HRManagement System

The University HR Management System is a Django-powered web app where authorities can self-register, authenticate, and onboard HR users through a secure form workflow. Once logged in, HR personnel manage faculty records—adding new instructors with join dates, departments, experience, and salaries; editing or deleting entries; and performing targeted searches by ID. The authority role oversees and updates faculty status (approved, rejected, pending) without exposing public endpoints, while automated database migrations and structured models (`Authority`, `HRUser`, `HR`, `Instructor`) ensure data integrity. With CSRF-protected forms, email-based login, role-guarded views, and clear URL routing for registration, login, status updates, and CRUD operations, this system delivers a robust, end-to-end solution tailored to university HR workflows.
